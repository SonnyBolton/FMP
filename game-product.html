Linked back to Research

I used my research on the game Peekaboo to help come up with the designs for my character models as in Peekaboo the character models are very low poly and blocky so when creating my character I followed the same approach and created a low poly female witch to use as the hunters. The game Peekaboo uses a simple art style using low poly and vibrant colours which heavily impacted my decision to use the same approach with my game creating all characters and models using this same low poly, vibrant colours.







Using my planning and research mood boards i used a similar art style keeping all the models low poly even though I didnt use the same colour scheme of using vibrant colours the contrast in model styles are as close as I could make them with some of the models being sourced. While creating this level I kept using my mood boards from research to help decide on decisions on how I want my level to look and how it should have an impact on the player.
